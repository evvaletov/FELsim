Here is a set of plots for your seminar. These show FELsim and COSY
INFINITY results for the undulator Twiss matching optimisation.

One limitation not shown: the three codes (COSY, FELsim, RF-Track)
converge to different quadrupole currents despite reaching similar Twiss
parameters at the undulator. This is expected given the different physics
models (1st-order matrices vs 3rd-order DA maps). The alignment between
codes has recently improved, mainly due to bug fixes in the RF-Track
adapter. RF-Track results are not included as that model is still a work
in progress -- let me know if you'd like me to add them.
